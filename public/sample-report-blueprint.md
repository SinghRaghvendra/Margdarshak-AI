## 10-Year Career Roadmap (Age-Specific for Product Manager)

### Year 1 (Age 22): Associate Product Manager
- **Description:** Focus on learning the ropes. You'll work under a senior PM, manage a small feature, write specifications, and analyze user data.
- **Expected Salary:** ₹8,00,000 - ₹12,00,000 INR
- **Suggested Courses:** Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO), "Inspired" by Marty Cagan (Book).
- **Key Activities:** Conduct user interviews, learn to use Jira, build relationships with engineers.

### Year 2 (Age 23): Product Manager I
- **Description:** Take ownership of a small product or a significant feature. You will be responsible for its roadmap and success metrics.
- **Expected Salary:** ₹12,00,000 - ₹18,00,000 INR
- **Suggested Courses:** Advanced Product Management (Coursera, Udemy), Data Analytics for PMs.
- **Key Activities:** Launch your first feature, present to stakeholders, learn A/B testing.
